# Getting Started with Thinkmay CloudPC

## Welcome to Thinkmay CloudPC!
Thinkmay CloudPC provides you with a powerful virtual desktop that you can access from anywhere. Whether you need a secure work environment or want to game on the go, your CloudPC is ready whenever you are.

## How to Access Your CloudPC
No downloads or installations are required! Simply open your web browser and log in.
* **Supported Browsers**: We highly recommend using **Google Chrome** or **Apple Safari** for the best experience.
* **Supported Devices**: Access your PC from a Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS, or Android device.

## Features at a Glance
* **Always Connected**: Enjoy a secure, reliable connection to your workspace or games.
* **High-Quality Video**: Crystal clear video streaming that automatically adjusts to your internet speed.
* **Use Your Accessories**: 
  * Plug in your **Microphone** for voice calls.
  * Connect a **Gamepad** (controller), or use our built-in **Virtual Gamepad** on mobile devices.
* **Mobile Friendly**: Full Touch and Multi-Touch support makes using your CloudPC on a phone or tablet feel completely natural.

## Servers Near You
To give you the fastest, lag-free experience, our servers are strategically located in:
* Ho Chi Minh City (HCM)
* Hai Phong (HP)

## Tips for the Best Experience
1. **Network Connection**: While our adaptive streaming technology works on many networks, a wired Ethernet or strong 5GHz Wi-Fi connection ensures the lowest latency and highest picture quality.
2. **Close Unused Tabs**: If you are playing games or doing intensive work, closing unused browser tabs on your local device can improve performance.

*Need Help? Please reach out to our support team if you experience any connection issues!*
