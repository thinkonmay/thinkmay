## 2. Immediate Strategic Diagnosis

Thinkmay is no longer only validating demand. With nearly **400M VND monthly revenue** and about **600 paid monthly users**, the business has meaningful traction. Marketing should now shift from proving that people want cloud PC/cloud gaming to improving:

1. Category ownership.
2. Repeat usage.
3. Retention and churn reduction.
4. Paid acquisition efficiency.
5. Local market trust.
6. Clear differentiation against global cloud gaming/cloud PC competitors.

A rough revenue-per-paid-user estimate:

- 400M VND / 600 paid users = about **666k VND per paid user per month**.

This is higher than the 300k VND Standard plan, implying one or more of:

- Some users buy higher plans.
- Some users buy add-ons or extra time.
- Heavy users contribute a large share of revenue.
- There may be meaningful plan mix beyond Standard.

Marketing implication: Thinkmay should not optimize only for cheapest acquisition. It should identify and target the users with the highest lifetime value.

The new plan/margin information strengthens this point: **Standard** is the volume product, but **Performance** appears to be the best economic product. Marketing should therefore use Standard as the accessible entry point, while intentionally upselling qualified users into Performance.

Estimated Facebook spend of **30–40M VND/month** against nearly **400M VND/month** revenue suggests paid acquisition is not currently consuming an extreme share of revenue, though exact CAC/ROAS still requires conversion tracking. Organic TikTok is strategically valuable because it produces demand without direct media spend; the real cost is founder/team creative time.

## 3. Recommended Positioning Architecture

Thinkmay should not choose only one label. It should use a **three-layer positioning system**.

### 3.1 Cloud gaming = acquisition keyword

Use **cloud gaming** for discovery, ads, SEO, TikTok hooks, and mainstream customer understanding.

Users understand or search for phrases such as:

- cloud gaming
- chơi game PC trên điện thoại
- chơi Black Myth: Wukong không cần PC mạnh
- chơi GTA V / FiveM trên laptop yếu
- cloud gaming Việt Nam

However, “cloud gaming” alone is generic and puts Thinkmay directly against NVIDIA GeForce NOW, Boosteroid, Xbox Cloud Gaming, and other large global platforms.

### 3.2 Cloud PC = product explanation

Once users land on the website or product page, explain that Thinkmay is more than game streaming:

> Thinkmay is not just a game stream. It is your own Cloud PC: save data, install games, keep progress, use apps, and continue later.

This is important because saved data / personal computer continuity is one of Thinkmay’s strongest product-model differentiators.

### 3.3 Quán net trên mây = emotional local brand

This is likely the most memorable Vietnamese positioning:

> Không cần PC mạnh. Mở quán net trên mây từ điện thoại hoặc laptop yếu.

It connects the service to an existing local behavior: going to an internet cafe to use a powerful PC.

## 5. Core Customer Segments

## 5.1 Mobile AAA gamers

### User need

They want to play high-end PC/console-style games from mobile devices. This is the dominant current behavior: about **70–80%** of Thinkmay users use the service on mobile.

Likely interests:

- Black Myth: Wukong
- GTA V / FiveM
- AAA story games
- Controller/mobile gaming experiments

### Marketing hook

> **Điện thoại cũng chiến Black Myth: Wukong được.**

This segment is especially strong for TikTok because proof can be visual and surprising.

## 5.2 Weak laptop gamers

### User need

They already own a laptop, but it cannot run demanding games.

### Marketing hook

> **Laptop văn phòng vẫn chơi được GTA V/FiveM, Black Myth, FC Online.**

This segment is especially strong for Facebook ads because users can understand the practical problem quickly.

## 5.3 Internet cafe replacement users

### User need

They understand the internet cafe model but want convenience at home or on existing devices.

### Marketing hook

> **Không cần ra net. Mở PC gaming trên mây ngay tại nhà.**

This is a natural Vietnamese positioning bridge.

## 5.4 Jakarta / Indonesia expansion users

### User need

Good latency exists in Jakarta, and Indonesia already represents about **10–20%** of current users. This is not just a speculative expansion market; there is already early traction.

### Strategic priority

Vietnam should remain the primary domination market, while Indonesia should be developed as a focused secondary expansion market. The recommended approach is:

- Do not split the whole company focus 50/50 yet.
- Keep Vietnam as the main revenue/category-ownership engine.
- Build Indonesia as a repeatable second-market playbook, starting with Jakarta.
- Localize the best-performing Vietnam mobile-gaming campaigns rather than creating a completely separate brand from scratch.

### Possible Indonesian positioning

> **Warnet di cloud.**

### Possible hook

> **Main game PC berat dari HP/laptop kentang.**

This market should use separate landing pages, creatives, and localized TikTok/short-form content rather than only translated Vietnamese copy.

## 7. Churn Problem: Smoothness and Latency

Founder-reported churn reason:

> Product is not smooth enough or has too much latency.

Marketing should be honest here. Do not promise “zero latency.” Instead, build trust through specificity.

### Recommended message

> **Mượt nhất tại Hà Nội, TP.HCM và Jakarta. Hãy test thử trước khi mua gói dài.**

### Recommended trust page

Create a public page:

> **Thinkmay có mượt không?**

Suggested sections:

- Best cities:
  - Hanoi
  - Ho Chi Minh City
  - Jakarta
- Recommended internet:
  - 30 Mbps+
  - 5GHz Wi-Fi or Ethernet
  - avoid weak 4G/5G during peak hours if unstable
- Best game types:
  - AAA story games
  - RPG
  - action adventure
  - casual online
  - creative/AI/study workloads
- Less suitable / risky game types:
  - ultra-competitive FPS/MOBA
  - games with strict anti-cheat
  - some Riot Games titles
- Test-before-buy CTA:
  - trial plan
  - latency test
  - Discord support

This kind of transparency should reduce support burden and increase trust.

## 8. Riot / Competitive Online Game Issue

Thinkmay should not hide the competitive online game issue. It should turn it into clear positioning and expectation-setting.

Recommended copy:

> **Một số game online có anti-cheat nghiêm ngặt như Riot Games có thể không hỗ trợ Cloud PC. Thinkmay phù hợp nhất cho game AAA, game offline/story, GTA/FiveM, FC Online, đồ họa, học tập và làm việc nặng.**

This is important because competitors like Shadow and airgpu already disclose anti-cheat / VM / cloud PC limitations. Thinkmay will look more trustworthy if it is honest.

### Recommended artifact

Create a public game compatibility list:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Works well | Good experience for most users in supported regions |
| Works with settings | Needs setup/network/settings guidance |
| Limited | Works sometimes or for some modes only |
| Not recommended | Bad latency fit or known issues |
| Blocked / unsupported | Anti-cheat, account, VM, or technical incompatibility |

## 17. Update: Paid Acquisition and Plan Economics

Founder update:

- The **Performance plan** has the best margin / strongest economic upside.
- Facebook ads spend is estimated at **30–40 million VND/month**.
- TikTok content is currently **organic only**; there is no TikTok ad spend, only team time.

### Implications

1. **Standard should remain the mass-market entry plan.**
   It is the best-selling plan and likely easiest for users to understand, so it should stay prominent in ads and landing pages.

2. **Performance should become the intentional upsell path.**
   Since Performance has the best margin, marketing should not only sell Standard. It should identify users who are more likely to need Performance:
   - Black Myth: Wukong players.
   - GTA V / FiveM users who play longer sessions.
   - users who care about higher graphics settings.
   - streamers/content creators.
   - AI/student/heavy-app users.
   - users who complain Standard is not smooth enough.

3. **Facebook ads should optimize for qualified paid users, not only cheap signups.**
   With 30–40M VND monthly spend, Thinkmay should track campaign performance by plan purchased, not just registration or first payment. A campaign that creates fewer Performance buyers may be better than a campaign that creates many low-retention Standard users.

4. **Organic TikTok should be treated as a creative testing engine.**
   The best organic TikTok hooks should be reused in Facebook ads. TikTok can discover what people emotionally respond to; Facebook can scale the proven hooks with paid spend.

### Recommended Facebook campaign structure with Performance upsell

- **Campaign 1: Standard entry / weak device pain**
  - Hook: laptop yếu / điện thoại vẫn chơi game PC.
  - Goal: affordable first purchase.
  - Landing plan highlight: Standard.

- **Campaign 2: Performance / AAA heavy game**
  - Hook: Black Myth, high settings, smoother heavy games.
  - Goal: sell Performance directly or upsell after trial.
  - Landing plan highlight: Performance.

- **Campaign 3: Retargeting trial and Standard users**
  - Hook: nâng cấp để chơi mượt hơn / cấu hình mạnh hơn / game nặng hơn.
  - Goal: upgrade to Performance.

- **Campaign 4: Indonesia/Jakarta mobile test**
  - Hook: main game PC berat dari HP / laptop kentang.
  - Goal: validate CAC and conversion by Indonesian users, starting in Jakarta.

### Recommended TikTok-to-Facebook workflow

1. Post organic TikTok videos around mobile proof, weak laptop proof, game-specific proof, and what-to-play-next recommendations.
2. Track which hooks get saves, comments, profile clicks, and Discord/website traffic.
3. Turn the best hooks into Facebook ad creatives.
4. Split ad destinations by intent:
   - Standard page for price-sensitive weak-device users.
   - Performance page for Black Myth/heavy-game users.
   - Compatibility/latency page for skeptical users.

### Strategic conclusion

Thinkmay’s funnel should become:

> **TikTok discovers demand and winning creative. Facebook scales conversion. Standard lowers the barrier. Performance captures margin.**

## 18. Update: Monthly Retention and Churn Diagnosis

Founder update:

- About **20%** of monthly subscribers renew for month 2.
- About **10–15%** renew for month 3.
- By month 6, about **5–7%** remain paying.

### Interpretation

This is the clearest growth bottleneck so far. Thinkmay has proven that users will pay, but most monthly users do not yet build a long-term habit around the service.

The retention curve implies:

- Month 1 → Month 2: roughly **80% churn**.
- Month 1 → Month 3: roughly **85–90% churn**.
- Month 1 → Month 6: roughly **93–95% churn**.

This does not mean the business is weak. It likely means the dominant use case is currently **event/game-driven** rather than habit-driven:

> User wants to play one specific heavy game → subscribes → finishes or loses interest → churns.

This matches the founder’s earlier churn reason: users do not know what to play next after finishing the main story.

### Strategic implication

Marketing should not only acquire more first-month users. Thinkmay needs a retention system that creates a second-month reason before the first month ends.

The key question becomes:

> **What makes a user decide to renew before their current month expires?**

Likely renewal triggers:

1. They are still playing a long game.
2. They discover the next game before finishing the current one.
3. They join a social/community loop.
4. They use Thinkmay for more than games: school, AI, rendering, work, storage.
5. They upgrade from Standard to Performance because they feel a quality difference.
6. Their setup becomes smooth enough that Thinkmay feels like a reliable habit.

### Recommended retention targets

Initial realistic targets:

- Month 2 renewal: improve from **20%** to **30–35%**.
- Month 3 renewal: improve from **10–15%** to **20–25%**.
- Month 6 retained payer: improve from **5–7%** to **10–12%**.

These targets are meaningful because even modest retention improvement can substantially increase LTV and allow higher CAC.

### Retention strategy: first 30 days

The first month should be treated as an onboarding campaign, not just a subscription period.

#### Day 0: Activation

Goal: first successful smooth session.

Actions:

- Show recommended games that work well.
- Ask user device/location: mobile/laptop, Hanoi/HCMC/Jakarta/other.
- Guide them to best settings.
- Push Discord/support if setup fails.

#### Day 3: Confidence

Goal: prevent early disappointment.

Actions:

- Send “setup mượt hơn” tips.
- Ask if they experienced lag.
- Recommend best games for their device.
- Encourage joining Discord.

#### Day 7: Discovery

Goal: introduce next game before boredom begins.

Actions:

- “Top games đang hot trên Thinkmay tuần này.”
- “Nếu bạn chơi Black Myth, hãy thử Elden Ring/Sekiro/Lies of P.”
- “Nếu bạn chơi GTA/FiveM, hãy thử Red Dead/Cyberpunk/open-world games.”

#### Day 14: Habit

Goal: create recurring use beyond one game.

Actions:

- Weekly game challenge.
- Discord event.
- Share setup/gameplay for rewards.
- Performance plan upsell for users playing heavy games.

#### Day 21: Renewal preparation

Goal: make month 2 feel planned.

Actions:

- Personalized next-game recommendation.
- Offer upgrade or renewal incentive.
- Show saved data / progress continuity.
- Remind what they lose if they churn: saved setup, installed games, ongoing progress.

#### Day 27–30: Renewal close

Goal: convert to second month.

Actions:

- “Your next month game list is ready.”
- Renewal bundle: continue playing + try recommended game.
- Performance upgrade prompt for heavy users.
- Discount carefully if needed, but avoid training users to wait for discounts.

### Product/content features to support retention

1. **Next Game Recommendation Engine**
   - Based on current/last played game.
   - Especially for Black Myth, GTA/FiveM, FC Online.

2. **Game Compatibility + Smoothness Score**
   - Show which games run well in Hanoi/HCMC/Jakarta.
   - Reduce bad-fit purchases and support burden.

3. **Monthly Game Calendar**
   - “Games to play this month on Thinkmay.”
   - Gives users a reason to stay subscribed.

4. **Mobile-first setup score**
   - Help mobile users optimize controls/network/settings.
   - Since 70–80% use mobile, this directly affects retention.

5. **Discord retention loops**
   - Game recommendations.
   - Weekly challenges.
   - User screenshots/videos.
   - Community votes for next featured game.

### Marketing implication

The biggest upside may not be reducing CAC. It may be increasing month-2 renewal.

If Thinkmay keeps acquiring users at the current pace but raises second-month renewal from 20% to 35%, the business gains more revenue from the same acquisition engine and can afford stronger ads, better creator content, and better infrastructure.

### Strategic conclusion

Thinkmay’s current retention curve confirms that the company should market not only as a way to play one specific game, but as:

> **A place where mobile/weak-device gamers always know what to play next.**

## 19. Bottom Line

Thinkmay should position itself with a layered strategy:

- **Cloud gaming** for acquisition and search.
- **Cloud PC** for product truth and differentiation.
- **Quán net trên mây** for emotional local memory and Vietnamese market fit.

The strongest near-term marketing opportunity is not just buying more ads. It is building a retention/content engine around:

> **What should I play next on Thinkmay?**

The biggest trust opportunity is to be more honest and specific about:

- latency,
- best cities,
- supported game types,
- anti-cheat limitations,
- and why Thinkmay is a full Cloud PC rather than only a game catalog.

If Thinkmay executes this well, it can own a stronger position than generic “best cloud gaming service”:

> **The mobile-first cloud internet cafe for Vietnam first, then Indonesia — a full Cloud PC for heavy games, weak devices, saved data, and local support.**
