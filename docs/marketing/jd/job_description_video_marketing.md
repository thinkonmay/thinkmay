# Job Description: Video & Visual Marketing Intern (CloudPC & Gaming)

## About Thinkmay

Thinkmay is a CloudPC platform—**quán net trên mây** for Southeast Asia—that streams full Windows gaming PCs to browsers on phones, laptops, and tablets. Our audience lives on **short-form video**, streams, and high-energy gaming visuals. We are hiring a **Video & Visual Marketing Intern** for work that needs **taste, timing, and personality**—AI can help, but the final cut still needs a human “soul.”

---

## Role Overview

You help with **motion and video**: shorts, explainers, gameplay edits, templates, and thumbnails. Founders set creative direction; you iterate from feedback until clips are ready to post.

**This role is not for you if** you mainly want to write SEO articles or keyword spreadsheets—that is the [Content & SEO Marketing Intern](./job_description_content_marketing.md) track.

## What You Will Do

### Short-form

- Edit TikTok / Reels / Shorts: hooks in the first second, captions, sound, pacing, gameplay + product moments.
- Learn what performs per platform (length, safe zones, silent-first viewing).

### Long-form & campaigns (support)

- Assist on explainers, community highlights, event recaps, Discord/stream clips.
- Practice simple arcs: problem → play → payoff on assigned projects.

### Visual identity

- Apply Thinkmay’s dark, high-energy gaming look (mobile play, controllers, instant play) under a shared style guide.
- Build/maintain reusable packs: lower thirds, end cards, transitions—not one-off AI slop.
- Coordinate with the **content intern** on words vs. visuals.

### Production habits

- Organize project files, aspect-ratio exports, and capture notes (including from Thinkmay Cloud PC sessions).
- Use AI for rough cuts, captions, or cleanup—you own **final rhythm and vibe**.

## Who Should Apply

### Must have

- **Student or recent graduate** (≈12 months or less) in film, multimedia, design, marketing, or strong self-taught practice.
- **Reel or portfolio** with **at least 3 video pieces** (school, personal, fan, or stream highlights OK). Gaming / tech / youth culture preferred. Rough edges are fine; **taste** must show.
- **One editor you know well** (Premiere, DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut, CapCut advanced, etc.). After Effects or motion is a plus, not day-one required.
- You can explain **why** a cut works (pacing, sound, hook)—not only that you “know CapCut.”
- **Reliable and coachable:** export revisions quickly, respect basic music/licensing rules we set.

### Critical — gaming credibility

- You are a **real gamer**. You have **seriously played at least one AAA title** (hours, online, or story)—not only casual mobile.
- In your application, **name the AAA game(s)** and what you played. Edits should feel like they come from someone who watches trailers and plays—not stock-footage energy.

### Nice to have

- Thumbnails, motion templates, or stream-highlight workflow.
- Vietnamese and/or Indonesian for on-screen copy and local humor.
- You post your own edits and pay attention to retention (learning mindset).

### Not required

- Agency years, brand-side lead credit, or a cinema-grade reel.
- Running full campaigns alone—we review in small loops.

### Usually not a fit

- No video samples to show.
- No real AAA play time and no authentic gaming references in interview.
- Only template/stock edits with no willingness to improve from notes.
