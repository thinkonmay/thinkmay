# Job Description: Content & SEO Marketing Intern (CloudPC & Gaming)

## About Thinkmay

Thinkmay is a CloudPC platform—**quán net trên mây** for Southeast Asia—that lets people run heavy PC games and demanding apps in a web browser, with no install, on phones, laptops, and tablets. Most of our users are mobile-first gamers in **Vietnam and Indonesia**. We are hiring a **Content & SEO Marketing Intern** for work that is structured, text-heavy, and a strong fit for **AI-assisted workflows** (with human review before anything ships).

---

## Role Overview

You help with **static, technical marketing**: SEO copy, FAQs, metadata, blog posts, and platform text. Founders set priorities and review your work; you learn by shipping small pieces often.

**This role is not for you if** your main passion is video editing, motion graphics, or “vibe” cuts—that is the [Video & Visual Marketing Intern](./job_description_video_marketing.md) track.

## What You Will Do

### SEO & landing pages (guided)

- Research keywords and intent for category terms (cloud PC, cloud gaming, thuê máy tính online, quán net trên mây, sewa cloud PC, etc.).
- Draft/update `vi`, `id`, and `en` titles, meta descriptions, headings, and body copy from briefs and [existing SEO docs](../seo/).
- Improve catalog, pricing, and FAQ pages using our templates and checklists.
- Note crawl/index issues on public pages; engineering implements fixes.

### Platform & static content

- Publish social captions, threads, carousel **text**, help snippets, and release summaries from briefs.
- Use AI for outlines and first drafts; **you** fact-check product claims, game names, and local tone before publish.
- Keep the content calendar, keyword list, and prompt templates up to date in shared docs.

### Conversion copy (learning)

- Draft onboarding and trial-to-paid lines (Google sign-in, cloud saves, mods, pause/rollover, VietQR / PayOS / Dana / OVO).
- Run small tests on headlines and CTAs when assigned; report results in a short weekly note.

## Who Should Apply

### Must have

- **Student or recent graduate** (≈12 months or less since graduation) in marketing, communications, journalism, business, IT, or similar—or clear self-taught proof (blog, fan page, club work).
- **Strong Vietnamese writing** (native or near-native) and **English good enough** to read docs and tools.
- **Interest in SEO and words**, not only pretty visuals—you care why a title ranks and why a CTA clicks.
- **Daily AI tool use** (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) with discipline: no blind copy-paste; you verify facts.
- **Organized and coachable:** hit agreed deadlines, keep edits in docs, respond in chat within a business day.

### Critical — gaming credibility

- You are a **real gamer**. You have **finished or seriously played at least one AAA title** (e.g. story progress, ranked/online hours, or long-term live service)—**not** only hyper-casual mobile.
- In your application, **name the AAA game(s)** you played and roughly how (hours, mode, platform). Generic “I like games” does not pass.

### Nice to have

- Samples: posts, Notion/Doc articles, school projects, or SEO homework—not a corporate portfolio required.
- Touched Google Search Console, Analytics, or Sheets.
- Indonesian language or market familiarity.
- Active in SEA gaming spaces (Discord, GTA V / FiveM, FC Online, etc.).

### Not required

- Full-time marketing years, agency background, or “senior SEO” title.
- Design or video skills (other intern track covers that).

### Usually not a fit

- You have never played a AAA game and cannot discuss one concretely in an interview.
- You only want to post memes with no interest in search, structure, or product accuracy.
- You resist edits or miss deadlines repeatedly during the first two weeks.
