### The AI-Era Edge: Why Join Us?

This is not a traditional internship where you do boring manual chores. At Thinkmay:
- **You will learn how to do marketing in the AI era:** Shift from a simple content producer to an AI orchestrator. You'll master how to guide, edit, and scale content/video using advanced AI tools while keeping the crucial human "soul" and brand quality.
- **You will get to know real agent-driven software development:** Work directly alongside founders and engineering. You will see firsthand how a modern tech product is built and shipped using autonomous AI coding agents, giving you a front-row seat to the future of software development.

### Shared rules

- **Gaming credibility (required):** You must have **seriously played at least one AAA title**. Name it in your application with real detail (mode, hours, platform). Marketing-only applicants who do not play games are not a fit.
- **Profile:** Students or recent grads (~12 months); personal or school work counts.
- **Internship:** ~3 months to start, ~20 h/week part-time or full-time in breaks; monthly VND stipend by hours; remote; weekly check-ins with founders.
- **Apply:** **huyhoangdo@contact.thinkmay.net** — use subject `Intern – Content SEO – [name]` or `Intern – Video – [name]` and follow the checklist in the JD you chose.
