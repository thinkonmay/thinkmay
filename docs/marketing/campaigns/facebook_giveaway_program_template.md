# Facebook Giveaway Program Template

_Date: 2026-05-21_

## Purpose

This template is designed for Thinkmay Facebook giveaway campaigns that aim to:

- increase qualified reach among gamers
- reward real Thinkmay users
- encourage account usage before entry
- reduce fake / low-quality giveaway participation

This version is tailored to a prize structure like:

- **1x phone-attached gamepad** (example: Gamesir X5 Lite)
- **1x Thinkmay Performance Plan**

and an entry logic based on:

- tagging a friend
- using a real Facebook account
- submitting a screenshot of the Thinkmay `/profile` page

---

## 1. Campaign objective

Before launch, define the main objective clearly.

Choose one primary goal:

- **Acquisition:** attract new relevant users
- **Activation:** get users to sign up and use Thinkmay at least once
- **Reactivation:** bring inactive users back
- **Community growth:** increase fanpage / Discord / gaming community engagement
- **Loyalty:** reward active users with usage history, rank, and streaks

### Suggested objective for this campaign

A good default objective is:

> Increase qualified gamer reach while encouraging real product usage through Thinkmay profile verification.

---

## 2. Prize fit check

A giveaway works better when the prize matches the audience.

### Recommended prize structure

- **Main physical prize:** Gamesir X5 Lite or similar mobile cloud-gaming accessory
- **Digital prize:** Thinkmay Performance Plan / play hours / account credit

### Why this works

- attracts actual gaming users instead of generic freebie hunters
- reinforces Thinkmay’s mobile and cloud-gaming positioning
- creates a link between product experience and reward

### What to avoid

Avoid prizes that are too generic, such as:

- cash
- random vouchers unrelated to gaming
- expensive electronics with no cloud-gaming connection

These often increase low-quality entries.

---

## 3. Entry structure

Use a two-stage structure:

### Stage A: Public entry

Participants must:

1. Follow the Thinkmay fanpage
2. Like the giveaway post
3. Comment on the post
4. Tag **1 real friend** who may be interested in cloud gaming

Optional extra prompt for better engagement:

- answer a question such as:
  - “Bạn muốn chơi game nào trên Thinkmay nhất?”
  - “Nếu có Cloud PC hôm nay, bạn sẽ chơi game gì đầu tiên?”

### Stage B: Verification

Participants must provide:

- a screenshot of the Thinkmay `/profile` page

The screenshot should show:

- account identity or username
- rank
- usage heatmap / streak / activity panel

This helps verify that the entrant is a real Thinkmay user and not just a giveaway-only participant.

---

## 4. How to use the `/profile` screenshot correctly

The `/profile` screenshot is useful because it:

- proves the participant has a Thinkmay account
- proves real product interaction
- highlights rank / activity / streak behavior
- lets the campaign reward users who actually engage with Thinkmay

### Best practice

Ask participants to:

- submit a **clear screenshot** of `/profile`
- **hide or crop sensitive information** if needed
- ensure the screenshot clearly shows rank and activity heatmap

### Privacy guidance

Do not require sensitive information to be publicly exposed.

If the profile page includes anything sensitive, instruct users to hide or crop:

- email address
- balance / wallet amount
- subscription billing information
- any internal or private details not required for verification

### Recommended workflow

Use one of these options:

#### Option A — Public comment + private verification
- participant comments publicly to enter
- shortlisted finalists or winners send `/profile` screenshot by inbox / Discord / form

#### Option B — Public comment + screenshot in comment
- only use if the profile page is safe to share publicly
- include clear instruction to crop sensitive data

### Recommended default

Use **public entry + private screenshot verification**. This is usually the safest option.

---

## 5. “Real Facebook account” rule

Do not use vague wording only. Define what “real account” means in practice.

### Suggested rule

A valid Facebook account should:

- not be newly created only for giveaways
- show normal activity or basic profile history
- not be obviously fake, empty, or spam-like
- not be part of bulk-tagging or repeated giveaway abuse

### Recommended wording

> Ban tổ chức có quyền loại các tài khoản Facebook ảo, tài khoản tạo mới chỉ để tham gia giveaway, tài khoản spam, hoặc tài khoản có dấu hiệu gian lận.

---

## 6. Bonus mechanics (optional)

You can use the profile page to add quality-based bonus logic.

### Safe bonus options

- extra consideration for active Thinkmay users
- extra consideration for visible recent activity on heatmap
- extra consideration for users with a usage streak
- extra consideration for users with higher rank

### Important caution

Do not make the campaign too exclusive unless the goal is loyalty only.

If you require a very high rank or long streak, you may reduce participation too much.

### Better model

Use this structure:

- **basic entry** for everyone who completes requirements
- **bonus advantage** for more active users

Example:

> Mọi người chơi đủ điều kiện đều được xét chọn. Người dùng có hoạt động gần đây trên Thinkmay, streak hoặc rank cao hơn có thể được ưu tiên trong vòng đánh giá cuối cùng.

---

## 7. Anti-fraud rules

Include these clearly in every campaign:

- one Facebook account per person
- one Thinkmay account per person
- duplicate entries may be removed
- fake / edited screenshots are invalid
- fake, spam, or giveaway-only accounts may be disqualified
- tag spam does not increase winning chances
- organizer has the right to verify information before awarding prizes

### Additional recommendation

Make it clear that:

- duplicate comments do not create extra entries unless stated otherwise
- edited evidence or mismatched account identity may lead to disqualification

---

## 8. Winner selection model

Choose one model before publishing.

### Option 1 — Random draw among valid entries
Best for fairness and simplicity.

### Option 2 — Random draw among valid entries + quality verification
Best when you want fairness but still filter out weak participants.

### Option 3 — Qualified selection
Best when you want to reward active users and community quality more than raw volume.

### Recommended model for Thinkmay

Use:

> **Random draw among valid entries, followed by verification of Facebook account and Thinkmay `/profile` screenshot before final confirmation.**

This keeps the process fair while protecting against abuse.

---

## 9. Prize claim process

State the prize claim flow in advance.

### Suggested process

1. Announce provisional winner publicly
2. Contact winner by inbox or ask winner to message page
3. Verify:
   - Facebook account used for entry
   - Thinkmay `/profile` screenshot
   - shipping/contact info
4. Confirm eligibility
5. Deliver prize

### Claim deadline

Set a clear deadline, for example:

- winner must respond within **48 hours**
- otherwise the result is void and another winner is selected

---

## 10. Shipping and delivery rules

For a physical prize like a gamepad, define:

- shipping locations covered
- who pays shipping
- expected shipping timeline
- what happens if the winner is outside the eligible area

### Recommended wording

> Giải thưởng vật lý chỉ hỗ trợ giao trong khu vực áp dụng của chương trình. Nếu người thắng ở ngoài khu vực hỗ trợ, ban tổ chức có quyền đổi sang phần thưởng tương đương hoặc chọn người thắng khác tùy theo thể lệ đã công bố.

For the Performance Plan, define:

- duration
- whether it is account credit or plan activation
- whether it is transferable or not
- claim deadline

---

## 11. Metrics to track

Do not evaluate the campaign by likes and comments only.

### Track at least:

- total reach
- total comments
- total valid entries
- number of entrants with valid Thinkmay `/profile` proof
- new Thinkmay signups during campaign
- number of entrants who start a session after entering
- number of Discord joins or community joins
- cost per qualified entrant
- cost per activated user

### Most important internal KPI

A strong KPI for this type of campaign is:

> **qualified entrants who become active Thinkmay users**

## 14. Strategic guidance

This giveaway structure is best when the goal is **qualified reach plus activation**, not just vanity engagement.

It works well because:

- the prize fits the product
- the profile screenshot filters low-quality participation
- rank / heatmap / streak create a feeling of real user identity
- the campaign can reward both community and activity

### Best use case

Use this format when you want to:

- attract gamers who are likely to use Thinkmay
- reward existing activity
- create social proof around Thinkmay’s user progression system

### Less ideal use case

Do not rely on this structure if your only goal is to maximize raw reach at the lowest friction possible, because the verification requirement will reduce entry volume.

---

## 15. Final recommendation

For Thinkmay, the recommended giveaway structure is:

- public Facebook entry for reach
- Thinkmay `/profile` screenshot for quality control
- private verification when possible
- clear anti-fraud rules
- performance tracking based on activated users, not comment count alone

This keeps the giveaway aligned with Thinkmay’s actual product and community instead of becoming a generic freebie campaign.
