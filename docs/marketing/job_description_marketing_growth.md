# Job Description: Growth Marketing Manager (CloudPC & Gaming)

## About Thinkmay
Thinkmay is an innovative CloudPC platform that makes high-end gaming and software accessible to anyone, on any device. We allow users to instantly run heavy PC games and demanding applications directly through a web browser on basic laptops, tablets, and smartphones—with zero downloads required. 

Our core user base consists of gaming communities across Southeast Asia (primarily Vietnam and Indonesia), with 70% of our customers playing on mobile devices. We are seeking a creative, analytical, and community-centric **Growth Marketing Manager** to lead our user acquisition and retention efforts, optimize conversion funnels, elevate our brand's online presence, and build organic search momentum in our key markets.

---

## Role Overview
As our **Growth Marketing Manager**, you will drive user growth and improve user lifecycle retention. You will own organic search visibility (SEO) in Vietnam and Indonesia, collaborate with product teams to refine the user onboarding and purchase funnel, ensure our website visuals match modern gaming aesthetics, and design engaging campaigns that keep our community active.

- **Reports to:** Head of Growth / Founders
- **Location:** Remote / Hybrid (Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam)
- **Target Markets:** Vietnam, Indonesia, and broader Southeast Asia

---

## Key Responsibilities

### 1. Organic Search & SEO Growth
* **Category Traffic Acquisition:** Scale our organic search visibility by shifting focus from branded search terms to high-volume category queries (e.g., `cloud pc`, `cloud gaming`, renting online PCs, and virtual internet cafes).
* **Localized SEO Campaigns:** Manage localized content and metadata structures across Vietnamese (`vi`), Indonesian (`id`), and English (`en`) pages to capture rising search interest in Southeast Asia.
* **Landing Page Conversion:** Improve the search performance and click-through rates (CTR) of our public pages (such as our game discovery catalog and pricing pages) by writing compelling, search-optimized copy and FAQs.
* **Crawl & Indexing Health:** Collaborate with the website team to ensure public marketing pages are indexable while secure user dashboards and private account utility pages are excluded from search results.

### 2. Funnel Optimization & Conversion
* **Onboarding Friction Reduction:** Design clear, compelling messaging for landing pages to drive signups through our quick, passwordless login options (e.g., 1-click Google sign-in).
* **Trial-to-Paid Conversion:** Create targeted campaigns prompting users on free trials to upgrade to paid tiers, focusing on the core value of persistent cloud storage (saving game progress, customized mods, and user data).
* **Friction & Churn Management:** Identify and address points of user drop-off. Refine promotional messaging around pricing structures (e.g., highlighting session pausing or rollover options) and support channels to mitigate billing concerns.
* **Checkout UX Support:** Work with product designers to simplify the purchase experience on payment screens, highlighting local digital wallets and gateways (VietQR, PayOS, Dana, OVO) and validating promotional discounts.

### 3. Website Design & "Vibe" Evolution
* **Premium Gaming Branding:** Ensure the website's public marketing assets reflect a modern, high-spec gaming lifestyle. Guide visual design toward a sleek, dark-mode-first aesthetic with energetic colors and modern typography.
* **Mobile-First Visuals:** Champion user-interface designs that showcase the product’s ease of use on mobile devices, emphasizing key features like our virtual onscreen gamepads and the ability to switch play instantly from a phone to a laptop.
* **Trust-Building Benefits:** Promote key customer advantages clearly on the homepage: instant play with no downloads, high-performance graphics, anti-cheat gaming safety, and support for physical controllers.
* **Privacy Messaging:** Craft clean, user-friendly copy explaining our commitment to privacy and how anonymous usage data is utilized solely to improve server routing and game recommendations.

### 4. Retention & Community Engagement
* **Community-Led Retention:** Run campaigns targeted at highly social gaming groups (such as multiplayer roleplay clans). Drive engagement via Discord streams, gaming events, and community setups.
* **Verified Giveaway Programs:** Execute campaigns where entries are verified through screenshots of user profiles (such as activity heatmaps or streaks) to incentivize real product usage and build community trust.
* **User Progression Campaigns:** Implement engagement programs that reward user loyalty, including streak rewards, usage milestones, and seasonal community challenges.

---

## Required Skills & Qualifications
* **Experience:** 3+ years of experience in growth marketing, product marketing, or SEO-focused marketing roles, preferably in B2C gaming, consumer SaaS, digital entertainment, or e-commerce.
* **SEO Management:** Proven experience planning and implementing SEO strategies, mapping keyword intent, writing optimized web copy, and managing search rankings in Vietnam and/or Indonesia.
* **Funnel Analysis:** Analytical mindset with the ability to track customer journeys and run campaigns targeting key performance indicators (`Clicks -> Signups -> Game Start -> Paid Upgrades`).
* **Gaming Culture Connection:** Strong understanding of Southeast Asian gaming communities, multiplayer games (e.g., GTA V roleplay, online sports games), and gaming communication hubs like Discord.
* **Creative Eye:** Ability to define brand style guidelines and collaborate with graphic designers and developers to build beautiful, highly interactive web pages.
* **Languages:** Native-level Vietnamese with professional English proficiency. Indonesian language skills or familiarity with the Indonesian gaming market is a strong advantage.
