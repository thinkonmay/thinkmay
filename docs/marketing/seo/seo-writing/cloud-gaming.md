## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is cloud gaming?
Cloud gaming is a technology where games are run on powerful remote servers in a datacenter, and the video feed is streamed to your local device. Your input (keyboard, mouse, gamepad) is sent back to the server in real-time.

### Do I need to buy the games?
Yes. Thinkmay provides the high-performance hardware, but you must own the games on platforms like Steam, Epic Games Store, or EA App. Simply log into your gaming account on the virtual machine.

### Is my connection secure?
Yes. Your virtual machine is completely isolated, and login sessions are protected. We use secure OAuth systems and automated VM cleanups to ensure user privacy.

### What internet speed do I need?
For a smooth 1080p gaming experience at 60fps, we recommend a stable internet connection of at least 15-20 Mbps. Using a 5GHz Wi-Fi band or a wired Ethernet cable dramatically improves stability.
